# Chatbot Usage Examples

## Quick Start

The chatbot appears as a floating button in the bottom-right corner of every page. Click it to start chatting!

## Example Conversations

### 1. Finding Products

**User:** "Show me t-shirts"
**Bot:** Lists available t-shirts with prices and sale information

**User:** "Do you have black shoes?"
**Bot:** Shows footwear products available in black

**User:** "What's on sale?"
**Bot:** Displays all products currently on sale with discount percentages

### 2. Size Inquiries

**User:** "What sizes do you have?"
**Bot:** Shows comprehensive size guide for clothing and footwear

**User:** "Size guide for shoes"
**Bot:** Provides detailed footwear sizing with US/EU conversions

**User:** "How do I measure my size?"
**Bot:** Gives step-by-step measurement instructions

### 3. Pricing Questions

**User:** "How much are your dresses?"
**Bot:** Shows price range for dresses ($89.99 - $119.99)

**User:** "What's the price range?"
**Bot:** Displays pricing for all categories

**User:** "Are there any discounts?"
**Bot:** Explains current sales and promotions

### 4. Shipping Information

**User:** "How long does shipping take?"
**Bot:** Explains delivery times (Standard: 5-7 days, Express: 2-3 days)

**User:** "Shipping costs?"
**Bot:** Details shipping fees and free shipping threshold

**User:** "Do you ship to Lagos?"
**Bot:** Confirms shipping options including same-day delivery

### 5. Returns & Exchanges

**User:** "Can I return this?"
**Bot:** Explains 30-day return policy

**User:** "How do I exchange for a different size?"
**Bot:** Guides through the exchange process

**User:** "Refund policy?"
**Bot:** Details refund timeline and requirements

### 6. Order Tracking

**User:** "Track my order"
**Bot:** Explains how to track orders and what status means

**User:** "Where is my order?"
**Bot:** Directs to order tracking resources

### 7. Payment Questions

**User:** "What payment methods do you accept?"
**Bot:** Lists all accepted payment methods (Cards, Paystack, Bank Transfer)

**User:** "Is payment secure?"
**Bot:** Explains security measures and encryption

### 8. General Help

**User:** "Help"
**Bot:** Shows all available assistance topics

**User:** "I have a question"
**Bot:** Offers quick reply options for common topics

## Quick Reply Buttons

The chatbot provides clickable quick reply buttons for faster navigation:

- **"Show me products"** - Browse product catalog
- **"Size guide"** - View sizing information
- **"Shipping info"** - Learn about delivery
- **"Track my order"** - Order tracking help
- **"Contact support"** - Get human assistance
- **"View sales"** - See current promotions

## Limitations

The chatbot is rule-based and works best with:
- Product inquiries
- Policy questions
- General information

For complex issues, the bot will direct you to:
- Contact support
- Your account dashboard
- Specific help pages

## Mobile Experience

The chatbot is fully responsive:
- Adapts to screen size
- Touch-friendly buttons
- Easy to minimize/close
- Doesn't interfere with browsing

## Accessibility

- Keyboard navigation supported
- Screen reader friendly
- High contrast text
- Clear button labels

## Privacy

- Conversations are client-side only
- No data sent to external services
- No conversation history stored (currently)
- No personal information required

## Getting More Help

If the chatbot can't answer your question:
1. Try rephrasing your query
2. Use the "Contact support" quick reply
3. Visit the FAQ page
4. Email: support@rudystore.com
